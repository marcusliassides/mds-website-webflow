03 / Motion

Motion principles.

  • Hover / lift — translateY(-2px), 180ms ease.
  • Page / fade-in — opacity 400ms ease-out.
  • Hero / slide — translateX + opacity 700ms cubic-bezier(.2, .7, .2, 1).
  • Hero interval — 7000ms.
  • Reduced motion — every animation collapses to 0.01ms when prefers-reduced-motion: reduce.
