About

Mattel, in games.

Mattel Digital Studios was formed to unify how Mattel shows up in games. The portfolio was already enormous — UNO had been shipping as a digital product since 2017, Hot Wheels had been a console fixture for a decade, creator-platform experiences were shipping across Roblox and Fortnite — but it had been decentralized.

MDS is the operating layer for all of it. We publish through Mattel163 and Mattel Games Studio, we license to the best partners in the business, and we expand Mattel brands onto creator platforms where a new generation finds them first. We work with a consistent set of questions: what makes this a great Mattel game, what makes it a great game full-stop, and where does a player already want to meet it?

The three pillars aren't just a strategy slide. They're how we make decisions about every title — from which studio should build it, to where it ships, to how it stays alive in market.
